The Problem
UIUCâ€™s Gies College of Business hosts experiential learning programs and courses to provide students with real world experience with corporate clients. These programs have grown significantly and as a result, our team was tasked to create a new content management system to connect all stakeholders involved.

The Goal
This is a project with significant process made with the majority of user research and initial prototyping completed. As a result, our team picked it up at the prototype stage where our main task was to understand what had been done so far, define what more needed to be designed for the MVP, then design these screen so that the prototype would be functional.


Final MVP


The Process
Our process began with a whiteboarding session where our team sat down to take a deep dive into what progress had been made in the project so far and what kind of tasks we needed to add. This session determined some of the key places in the site we should begin building off of namely, student profile set up, recruitment, global search feature, among others. This also helped us brainstorm questions for our client at Gies in order to clarify the scope the MVP should cover. Take a look at some ideas and topics we touched on:



As our team worked through protoyping different features, we also made sure to meet with the student leadership team that leads one of the experiential groups at Gies that would be using iControl, Illinois Business Consulting (IBC). This meeting was invaluable as through a series of questions, we were able to understand their side of running the program and therefore what we can do to make iControl as useful to them as possible.
